Formats

CDs, or Compact Discs, first became mainstream in the late 1980s to early 1990s. They provided more portability and were easier to use than vinyls. They are cheaper than vinyls, normally in the $20 to $30 range for a standard album.

Wikipedia Page

Records, also known as vinyl, were the first format of music that was very available and had a high fidelity (quality). Vinyls quickly became popular around 1950s, and many people still collect and use records in 2026.

Wikipedia Page

Digital music is the easiest way to listen to music. All you have to do is press a button and your music plays instantly. Most people use streaming services to listen to music due to its ease of use.
